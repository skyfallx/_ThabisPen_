Thoughts

They flow like a rive,
bringing boats filled
with the memories of us

I remember every moment
As i shed these tears waiting to fall.
They say love with your heart,
But take your mind with you.
My heart wanted you gone
but my mind kept you caged
what more could i ask for?

I thought that everything would be fine,
But the thoughts of you
Kept flooding in like the great tsunami in 2004 Indonesia.
My life was a highway,
That led nowhere but forward and backward.
I kept going backwards,
With the hope of finding you

But life had other plans.
Now all I have are these memories of you,
Prisoned in my mind with no chance of getting out.

The thoughts of you
Keep flowing like a river,
Bringing more old memories of us
With no aim of being forgotten.
