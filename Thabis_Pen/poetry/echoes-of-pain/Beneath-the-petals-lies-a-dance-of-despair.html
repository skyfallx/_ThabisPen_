Beneath the petals lies a dance of despair

She moves with grace, a mournful sway,
A dancer's form, but a face that's astray.
Into the shadow's, her identity resides,
A heart that aches with pain and despair inside.

The petals mask what the heart cannot express,
A flower that blooms, with its roots, lifeless.
She years to be seen, to be understood,
But the mask she wears is a fragile hood.

With every step, she searches for a way,
To escape this misery, which holds her each day.
But like a flower, she is rooted in her pain,
A blooming beauty but decaying in pain.

Longing to be noticed, she dances with all her might,
A fragile, fading light, in the dark of the night.
Her heart beating in sorrow and her soul with despair,
But still she dances, a beauty beyond compare.
