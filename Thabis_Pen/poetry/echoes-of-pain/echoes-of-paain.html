Echoes of pain

When the tears stopped falling, I thought that it was the end. Only to find out that... The echoes don't need tears to be heard.
I've come to learn that Pain remembers What the heart forgets And when it's quiet, It starts to creep in Bit by bit.
In the silence, It whispers to the ears, A slideshow to the mind Of every tear, Every scar Every cry
But I've grown out of it, I've grown to realize that All doesn't come back to haunt me, But to remind me, Of how far I have come, Of what I've survived, Of the strength I had built along the way.
And because of time I've come to accept it as is. I don't resist it anymore I listen to it, I listen to the echoes of pain.
