Echoes Of Pain

A poetry anthology about love, heartbreak and lost dreams.In this anthology,


Thabitha speaks her words from the heart, sharing her journey of hurt, healing and
finding yourself again. This serves as a reminder that even in pain, something very
beautiful can grow. Like a lotus, these poems bloom from the hardest and roughest
places, aimed to show that survival can too be beautiful. Read more

The girl I buried twice (In progress)

"Some memories are better left buried. Cassie? She never expected hers to dig
themselves up like this."

Six years ago, Cassie managed to survive a very traumatic event that left her with
no memories of it. One of the memories being her childhood love, John, a man she
once trusted with her life. As a newly qualified forensic psychologist starting a
promising career in a new town, she is set to rebuild her life, better this time. But
when she mistakenly comes across a pair of familliar eyes watching her, something

What lies behind her eyes (coming soon)

"What if YOU are the real threat?"

Amilia Pridz wakes up in the hospital with no memories of the past six months. Her


fiance sitting right next to her, holding her hand, gentle. Her friends are distant, too

That she fell and she is safe now. But something in her refuses to believe this. As
she pieces everything together, her shattered memories and the ocnfusing stories,
she discoveres a Glimpes of a devastating event. A name, scream, fire. She doesn't

Fear fears the fearless (coming soon)

But how do you heal when love is turned into a weapon

Nosipho Makhaya, a woman who marries young and dreams of forever. But who


knew that twelve years into her marriage, betrayal would shatter her trust when her
husband Thabang, the man she once loved deeply, cheats with someone close to
her heart, and turns abusive. Bound by hope and fear, Nosipho stays until her 30th
birthday, when she finds the strength to walk away. Fear fears the fearless is a story
of pain, resilience, and rediscovery. Nosipho's path is not just about survival, it's
about rising, healing, and inspiring others. Because sometimes, fear learns to fear
the one who refuses to be broken. Read more

I fell in love with a demon (coming soon)

Out of boredom and curiosity, Samantha Combs performs a forbidden ritual from a
book she found at an antique store, and unknowingly binds herself to a mysterious


female demon. What begins as fear soon becomes fascination, and then something
more: love. But love between a human and a demon is taboo. When her family
discovers the truth, they try to "save" her with a secret ritual, banishing her lover
forever. Haunted by the loss and the inability to move on, she finds her self thinking
of two options: live without love as she thinks she will never find anyone like her, or
die to be with her again. I Fell in Love with a Demon is a tragic sapphic tale of
forbidden desire, spiritual oppression, and the painful beauty of choosing your own
fate. Read more
